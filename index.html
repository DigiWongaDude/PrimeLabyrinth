The idea

Rooms, doors, primes, and decision points

Every chamber in the Prime Labyrinth is defined by a trio of numbers — its prime signature. Doors connect these signatures, hinting at the arithmetic that shaped them. Stepping through a door commits you to a new combination.

Some doors split your path in curious ways, others converge threads you left behind. The thrill comes from watching the breadcrumb trail build — a proof of your personal traversal through the maze.

Choices are rarely linear: pauses, reversals, and surprises turn the walk into a story. The Labyrinth rewards anyone willing to think in primes and embrace the branching narrative.
